# 14. Recommended Delivery Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Corporate launch

Public company website, services, projects, team, blog, contact, quotation, careers, legal pages and responsive design.

## Phase 2 — Operational administration

Content management, lead management, quotation workflow, recruitment, settings, user accounts and role access.

## Phase 3 — Business growth

Email notifications, CRM integration, analytics, newsletter, media library, project case studies, client portal and online appointment booking.

## Phase 4 — Enterprise integration

Customer dashboard, proposal acceptance, invoices, payment integration, project progress tracking, support tickets, WhatsApp/SMS integration and multilingual English/Odia content.
