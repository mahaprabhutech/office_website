# 8. Admin User Guide

## Login
Open `/admin/login` and sign in with an authorised administrator account.

## Dashboard
Review services, projects, new enquiries, quotations, job applications and recent activity.

## Services
Add title, summary, description, benefits, workflow, image, featured status and active status.

## Projects
Add category, overview, features, technology stack, status, links and gallery images.

## Enquiries
Assign a sales user, add notes, schedule follow-up and update the sales status.

## Careers
Publish jobs, review applications, download resumes and update candidate status.

## Job Applications
Review candidate details, download the stored resume, add internal notes and update recruitment status.

## Quotations
Review customer scope and platform requirements, record an estimate, attach a proposal reference and update the opportunity status.

## Users and Roles
Create individual administrator accounts and assign the minimum role required for their work.

## Settings
Update company contact information, hero copy, public statistics, social links, SEO defaults and footer content.

## Security
Use individual accounts, never share passwords, deactivate former staff accounts and change the seeded password.
