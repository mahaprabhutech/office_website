# 2. Modules and Features

## Public website

### Home
Branded hero banner, company introduction, featured services, featured projects, statistics, technology capabilities, testimonials, latest articles and contact call-to-action.

### About
Company profile, vision, mission, values, leadership, milestones and registration information.

### Services
Service list and detail pages, benefits, process, technology details, featured status, SEO slug and quotation action.

### Projects and products
Project categories, detail pages, features, technology stack, screenshots, brochure links, application links and project status.

### Team
Leadership and employee profiles, designation, department, skills, LinkedIn, ordering and active status.

### Careers
Vacancies, job descriptions, deadline, application form, resume upload and recruitment status.

### Blog and news
Articles, company news, categories, tags, featured image, draft/published status and SEO information.

### Contact and quotation
Contact enquiry, service selection, budget, project requirements, attachments and reference numbers.

## Admin panel

### Dashboard
Counts, recent enquiries, recent quotations, application totals and quick actions.

### Content management
Create, edit, publish, reorder and deactivate services, projects, team members, jobs, articles and testimonials.

### Enquiry management
Assign staff, add internal notes, set follow-up time and update status: New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted or Closed.

### Recruitment
Publish vacancies, accept public resume uploads, review applications, download resumes and update status: Received, Shortlisted, Interview, Selected or Rejected.

### Quotation management
Review project requirements, platforms and budget; record estimated amount, proposal path, internal notes and decision status.

### Users and roles
Create individual administrator accounts, assign Super Admin, Admin, Editor, HR, Sales or Viewer role, and activate or deactivate access.

### Settings
Company information, contact data, social links, SEO defaults, homepage copy, statistics and footer details.

## Roles
Super Admin, Admin, Editor, HR, Sales and Viewer.
